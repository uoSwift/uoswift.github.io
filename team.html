Who We Are

uoSwift was created by university students, for university students. Our mission is to provide a safe, reliable, and affordable ridesharing service tailored to the unique needs of campus life. We understand the challenges of student schedules and budgets, and we’re here to help you get where you need to go—swiftly and stress-free.

Our Services

With uoSwift, students can quickly find rides to and from campus, around town, or for group trips with friends. Our app offers flexible options, including ride pooling to reduce costs, safe rides to late-night events, and easy, instant payment. Whether you’re heading to a study session, a campus event, or just need a quick lift across town, uoSwift has you covered.
