Overall Look and Feel of uoSwift
===============================

The overall look and feel should be minimalist and flat since that is the more modern trend in web design.

Colours
========
Our colours will be close to the uOttawa colours, since our target audience are university students.

- #0b090a
- #161a1d
- #660708
- #a4161a
- #ba181b
- #e5383b
- #b1a7a6
- #d3d3d3
- #f5f3f4
- #ffffff

Iconography
===========
uoSwift will have flat icons since, again, this style is more modern in web design

Typography
==========
We will be using Inter from Google Fonts.

Links
=====
Links will not be underlined and should change color when a user hovers over it.

Buttons
=======
Buttons will be flat designed using the chosen colors outlined above.
