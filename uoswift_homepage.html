About UOswift

UOswift is a ride-sharing service exclusively for University of Ottawa students. We provide a unique platform that tailors to users' individual characteristics, matching them with like-minded peers for a more comfortable and enjoyable ride experience. All drivers are also uOttawa students, ensuring a trusted and student-friendly environment.

Our Services

At UOswift, we specialize in connecting uOttawa students for convenient, reliable, and safe ride-sharing options. Our service matches you with fellow students based on shared interests or preferences, creating a personalized and community-oriented transportation experience. We aim to enhance your campus commute while building meaningful connections.
